## 5.3 Processing of Buccal Swabs in Preparation for PCR

### 5.3.1 Materials Needed

* Water bath with thermometer
* Hot plate
* Circular holder for microcentrifuge tubes
* Tweezers
* Gauze squares
* Timer
* 1.0 M Tris-Cl buffer, pH 8.0
* Transfer pipettes
